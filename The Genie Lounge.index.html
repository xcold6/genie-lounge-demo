Our Story

Where Great Food Meets the Lounge Life

Tucked on Albert Place in the heart of Peterborough, The Genie Lounge blends the warmth of Turkish and Middle Eastern grilling with a relaxed, social atmosphere that's made for evenings worth remembering.

From sizzling mixed grills and sharing platters to creamy desserts, craft mocktails and premium shisha — this is a space designed for groups, families, and anyone who believes that good food deserves great company.
