About Spire Home Care

East London's Trusted Home Care Specialists

We are a team of highly dedicated, fully trained carers and office staff. Spire Home Care is a domiciliary care provider regulated by the Care Quality Commission (CQC), providing compassionate support so people can remain safely in their own homes.

Based in Ilford, Essex, we serve families across Redbridge and surrounding areas — delivering person-centred care that preserves dignity, independence, and quality of life.

  • Regulated by the Care Quality Commission (CQC)
  • All carers fully trained & DBS checked
  • Free initial care assessment
  • 24-hour emergency care support line
  • Person-centred, individual care plans
  • Partnered with NHS and Local Authorities

What We Offer

Our Care Services

We pride ourselves on working closely with our clients and families to deliver tailored care across a range of needs.

🏠

Home Care

Regular visits to support daily living, helping clients stay comfortable and safe in their own home.

🛁

Personal Care

Dignified, respectful help with bathing, dressing, grooming and personal hygiene routines.

🧠

Dementia Care

Specialist support for those living with dementia, delivered by trained carers with empathy and patience.

🤝

Companionship Care

Meaningful social interaction, outings and emotional support to combat loneliness and isolation.

🌙

Night Care

Overnight support giving families peace of mind with a dedicated carer through the night.

🛏️

Live-in Care

A full-time live-in carer providing round-the-clock support as a real alternative to residential care.

💛

End of Life Care

Compassionate, dignified palliative care allowing loved ones to remain at home in their final days.

💊

Medication Support

Safe, accurate assistance with medication management and health monitoring routines.
